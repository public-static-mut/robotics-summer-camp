The camp experience

Built for beginners, rewarding for returning robotics kids.

Every day blends guided instruction with real design work, so campers stay active, challenged, and proud of what they build.

Track 01

Build & Drive

Learn chassis assembly, motor control, sensors, and the basics of robot movement through fast, tactile projects.

Track 02

Code & Tune

Use beginner-friendly coding to make robots follow paths, respond to obstacles, and complete challenge missions.

Track 03

Team & Compete

Campers finish with mini-matches, scouting, and strategy sessions led by a team that has competed and won in SoCal.
